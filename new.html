THEME OF MEALBOX

HOMELY

Homely meal enable you to avoid innumerable additives and preservatives that go into food now a days.At Mista, we use natural ingredients to prepare food and it always gives you a delicious taste. Likewise, we are going to hire skillful home cooks from the locality to prepare homely food for our customers.

HEALTHY

The groundwork of all happiness is health, when you’ve got your health, you got everything. At Mista, utmost priority is given to ensure that the food we serve is as clean and healthy. We assign skillful home cooks so you can have delicious food of those hands which cares for your health and nourishment.

HYGIENE

Hygiene while preparing food is an important and essential thing mostly in this pandemic situation. At Mista, the home kitchens we entrust with are compliant with sanitization guidelines. We consistently organize taste and hygiene tests to make sure that through every meal, we deliver a moreish plateful of nutrition that even your mother would approve of !
